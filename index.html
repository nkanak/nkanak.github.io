Short CV

I am a Ph.D. Candidate at the Department of Mechanical and Aeronautical Engineering at the University of Patras (Industrial Management and Information Systems Laboratory, supervisor: Nikos Karacapilidis).

I hold a BSc. in Computer Science from the Department of Informatics and Telematics, Harokopio University of Athens.

My research aims to exploit existing techniques and methods from the fields of recommender systems, knowledge management, machine learning, operations research and digital personal assistants in order to enhance Project Management.

I am interested in recommender systems, applied machine learning and operations research.


I am currently studying techniques related to knowledge graphs and graph databases (in particular, improving text classification using graph-based text representation methods).
